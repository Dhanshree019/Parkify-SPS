Automatic Number plate recognition

Simply drive up to the barrier or entry gate of a parking lot. No need to collect parking tickets or pay on the spot.

What is ANPR?

Automatic number plate recognition, otherwise known as ‘licence plate recognition’, is technology that uses optical character recognition to read vehicle registration plates.You can use Parkable’s ANPR to grant secure, seamless entry and exit through access control gates, and record which vehicles are in your car park.

Why implemented Parkify?
Seamless access

Open access control simply by driving to the entrance. With no swipe cards needed, you have low-touch, easy access, removing congestion at the entry.

Heightened security

Limit car park access to an approved list of vechicles. Cameras record each vechicle so you know who has accessed your car park and for how long.

Automated reporting

ANPR cameras track which vechicles are in the car park, giving you automated, real-time information and reporting through the Parkable admin panel.
